Best for / not for

FAQ

What are the main things to see in Barcelona?

For many first-time visitors: Sagrada Família, Gaudí architecture, Passeig de Gràcia, Gothic Quarter, food, viewpoints and neighbourhoods.

Is a walking tour a good first activity?

Yes, if it gives context and helps organize the rest of the trip.
