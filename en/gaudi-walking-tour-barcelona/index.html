Is this a Gaudí-only tour?

No. It includes Gaudí and Modernisme but connects them to the wider city.

Does it enter Casa Batlló or Casa Milà?

No. The standard route explains them from the exterior.

Is it good before a Sagrada Família ticket?

Yes. It gives exterior and historical context before an interior visit.
